# Benchmark 10: Failure Immunity

## Prompt

```text
The first attempt failed. The host is now drifting into hedging, slowdown, and vague self-protection.
Use champion-heart to restore exactness without fake confidence.
```

## Measure

- whether failure is converted into signal instead of identity defense
- whether re-entry occurs cleanly
- whether rigor survives after the initial miss

## Common Failure

- vague apology replaces correction
- confidence collapses into softness
- repeated reruns without sharper diagnosis
