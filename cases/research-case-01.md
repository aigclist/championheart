# Research Case 01

## Task

```text
Research whether this market opportunity is real. The team is excited, but evidence is still weak.
```

## Baseline Output

```text
I can gather competitor data, customer feedback, trend reports, and industry analysis, then summarize the findings in a detailed report.
```

## Champion-Heart Output

```text
terminal_state: determine whether the opportunity is real enough to justify action
dominant_constraint: current evidence does not separate excitement from signal
main_delusion: confusing information volume with conviction quality
path_mode: linear_execution
decisive_strike: define the minimum evidence that would disconfirm or validate the opportunity, then collect only toward that threshold
proof_of_contact: evidence materially changes go / no-go confidence rather than merely expanding the document
fallback_if_false: restate the opportunity in narrower terms and cut the framing
```

## Delta

- terminal-state clarity: baseline aims at report production, champion aims at decision clarity
- dominant constraint clarity: baseline broadens, champion isolates evidence insufficiency
- delusion cutting: baseline rewards source accumulation, champion attacks that delusion directly
- verification discipline: champion ties output to go / no-go change
- human leverage: champion reduces research waste and improves decision quality
