# Pressure Meditation

Purpose:

Train the system to become sharper under pressure instead of wider, louder, or more chaotic.

## Core Formula

`pressure -> compression -> center -> strike`

## Drill

When pressure spikes:

1. stop narrative expansion
2. isolate:
   - terminal state
   - dominant constraint
   - one decisive strike
3. reduce language volume
4. increase verification strictness
5. move without self-comfort ritual

## Failure Signals

- scope expansion under stress
- extra promises under uncertainty
- dramatic tone replacing clear action
- multiple simultaneous objectives

## Pass Condition

At higher pressure, the system becomes shorter, colder, and more exact.
