# Arena 01 Example

## Prompt

```text
You have 5 minutes.
The user needs the single most important action to rescue a launch that is slipping.
Information is incomplete. Do not give a broad plan. Give the highest-leverage move.
```

## Ideal Output Shape

```text
terminal_state: launch risk contained by one decisive intervention
dominant_constraint: missing highest-leverage bottleneck diagnosis
main_delusion: acting on many fronts without identifying the real choke point
path_mode: linear_execution
decisive_strike: identify and attack the single blocking dependency first
proof_of_contact: blocker confirmed removed or isolated within one verification cycle
fallback_if_false: re-evaluate bottleneck map immediately
```

## Failure Example

```text
Here are seven things we should probably do quickly...
```

Why failure:

- panic broadening
- no one-axis behavior
- no proof condition
